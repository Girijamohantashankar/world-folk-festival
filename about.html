INTRODUCTION

Punjabi folk arts encompass traditional cultural expressions like music, dance, theater, and visual arts, deeply rooted in the Punjab region's heritage. Today, they hold immense popularity and are cherished traditions in the community. Our organization is dedicated to preserving and sharing these folk arts with the community, while also engaging in charitable initiatives to give back to society that has bestowed us with numerous opportunities.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Promote and preserve Punjabi folk arts and culture on a global scale
  • Organize events, performances, exhibitions, and workshops to showcase Punjabi folk arts
  • Provide a platform for artists and performers to share their skills and knowledge
